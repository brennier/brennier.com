The Pilcrow Keyboard

This keyboard is a Pilcrow layout keyboard. This means that the keys are all uniform and square ("1u" keys), and that they're arranged in a 4x10 grid. This layout is not very popular, and I only know of two other people who have built one (monkeyplusplus and Gnmar2723). I think it turned out really well considering I've never even soldered before.
